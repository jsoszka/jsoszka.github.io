Veterinary Locum Rates Survey

This website aims to provide locum professionals with up-to-date insight into the locum veterinary market, I will run this survey and update the report with the latest data on a monthly basis, eventually showing trends to locum rates, number of days worked and realistic turnover a locum can expect.

As there will be a new survey every month, please subscribe to get notified on a monthly basis when the new survey and results are released.

The data provided via the surveys will only be used to publish reports, available to everyone for free, raw data isn't shared with anyone else.
